Schizophrenia

Schizophrenics

There are many effects for Schizophrenics. They are mostly behavioral, cognitive, and psychological. The effects are all mental but there can also be major physical effects. Down below there are some of the aspects of having Schizophrenia.

Behavioral

People with Schizophrenia tend to socially isolate themselves, they also think that their thoughts they aren't theirs. They can also have compulsive behavior, hostility, and self-harm or suicidal thoughts. Suicide is also a open factor often.

Cognitive

Schizophrenia can have cognitive disorders too, like amnesia, delusion, thought disorder, etc. They can also think an ordinary event has a personal and special meaning. They also may have a hard time in school or learning new things.

Phsychological

With the Phsycololgical effects of Schizofrenia, there is a possibility to have hallucination, paranoia, hearing voices, depression, and even religious delusions. People with Schizophrenia can also see things that other poeple cant or don't see.
