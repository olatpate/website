Cures/Treatments

There are no known direct cures but there have been people who have recovered from Schizophrenia by things like Therapy, Hospital Visits, Pills, Counciling, Psychoeducation, and other things.

Some drugs and medicine include Anti-Tremor and Antipsychotic medicines and drugs. You can also have a Clinical Psychologist and a Psychiatrist. The pills take away all of your personality so the Schizophrenia goes away but can also take everything else with it like memories and normal emotions.
